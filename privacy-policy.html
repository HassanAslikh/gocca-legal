Privacy Policy for GOCCA

Last updated: February 2025

GOCCA (“the App”) helps CCAs track and deliver misrouted packages and parcels. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Email (Firebase Authentication)

We collect your email address to authenticate your account, verify your identity, and secure your data.

1.2 Camera Access

The app uses your camera only to take photos of package labels to extract address information. GOCCA does not store or share these photos.

1.3 Device Location

GOCCA uses your device’s location to calculate the distance between your current position and a package’s destination. Location data is not permanently stored or shared.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate and secure user accounts
  • Extract address information from captured images
  • Calculate delivery distance
  • Improve app performance and functionality

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase Authentication to manage login and user identity. Firebase may collect basic device information.

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. Data Security

We use secure communication (HTTPS) and rely on Firebase’s industry-standard security to protect your information.

5. Permissions

  • Camera – Capture package label images
  • Location – Calculate delivery distance
  • Internet – Authenticate using Firebase

6. Children’s Privacy

GOCCA is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect children’s data.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us to request access, updates, or deletion of your information.

Your information and data will be lost by uninstalling the app from your device

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will appear here with a new date.
